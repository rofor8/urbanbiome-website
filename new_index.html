Our Mission & Values

We strive to improve urban environments by integrating nature, applying permaculture thinking, and leveraging data to create sustainable, resilient ecosystems.

At our core, humans have a deep, instinctual need to connect with the natural world. Modern city life, for all its advantages, can often sever this connection. Our philosophy is simple: we believe the best urban spaces are those that honor this innate need for nature by weaving timeless, natural elements back into the city fabric.

Our Services

Offering expertise in creating resilient and ecologically sound urban spaces.

Nature-Based Solutions

Implementing green infrastructure like rain gardens, green roofs, and permeable pavements to manage stormwater and enhance urban biodiversity.

Permaculture Design

Creating sustainable human settlements and agricultural systems modeled on the relationships found in natural ecosystems.

Urban Greening Strategy

Developing comprehensive strategies for increasing green cover in cities, utilizing Open GIS data for site selection and impact assessment.

Solutions on the Ground

Discover practical ways UrbanBiome is making a difference, from community gardens to innovative green infrastructure.

Nature-based Solutions (NbS) leverage the power of nature to address urban challenges. Our research focuses on identifying and promoting effective NbS such as urban green spaces, green roofs, and sustainable drainage systems (SuDS). We also empower communities with DIY guides for projects like green roof installation, urban planters, and living walls.
